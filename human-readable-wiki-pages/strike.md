#Strikes

Huwans are not machines. If life in your city gets bad enough, they will stop working for you until things improve. This is called a **Strike**.


##When does a Strike happen?

Every caste has two happiness thresholds that determine when they go on strike:

- **Strike begins:** Once happiness drops below this value, some inhabitants start to refuse work. The strike starts small but grows the further happiness falls.
- **Full strike:** Once happiness drops below this value, the strike is at its maximum severity.

You can look up these thresholds for each caste on their respective caste page.


##What does a Strike do?

A strike has two effects:

**1. Workers stop showing up.**

Striking inhabitants will leave their jobs at nearby factories. The more severe the strike, the fewer are willing to work. The factories will try to fill those spots from other, non-striking houses nearby - but if those aren't available, production slows down or stops entirely.

**2. Caste bonus output drops.**

Even huwans that aren't employed in a factory normally contribute to their caste bonus through their everyday work. Striking inhabitants stop doing that too. For most castes, this drops to zero during a full strike. Some early castes retain a reduced caste bonus output even during a full strike.


##Can some workers still show up?

For a few castes, yes. The [caste=orchid] and [caste=clockwork] castes have a small fraction of their members who will keep working regardless of how unhappy they are. This is the **minimum worker fraction** - visible on their caste pages.

For all other castes this fraction is zero: a full strike means no one comes to work.


##How to end a Strike

Get their happiness back up above the 'strike begins' threshold. Strikes resolve automatically once happiness recovers - there is no lasting grudge. The usual suspects for low happiness are:

- Food supply problems (too little variety, wrong taste, no food at all)
- Water supply problems
- Poor housing comfort or mismatched housing traits
- Low health or sanity dragging happiness down through their respective multipliers

Check the Details tab of the affected house for a breakdown of what is pulling happiness down.
